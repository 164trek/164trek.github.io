Itinerary

6 days, 5 nights

Day 1

Friday, 10 July 2026

Fly from Singapore to Lombok. Transfer to Sembalun. Hotel check-in and trek briefing.

Day 2

Saturday, 11 July 2026

Trek from Sembalun to the crater rim. Camp overnight.

Day 3

Sunday, 12 July 2026

Early summit push. Descend to the lake and hot spring. Camp overnight.

Day 4

Monday, 13 July 2026

Descend through Torean to Senaru. Hotel stay.

Day 5

Tuesday, 14 July 2026

Visit waterfalls. Boat to Gili Island. Snorkeling and recovery time.

Day 6

Wednesday, 15 July 2026

Boat back to Lombok. Airport transfer and flight home.
