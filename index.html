Design principles

No falling risk

Walking trails only. Altitude, scree, weather, mud are fine. Ropes, exposed ridges, glaciers are out.

Start small

One mountain first, then the group decides what's next.

Prepared, not rushed

We build the fitness to finish comfortably and enjoy the whole way up.

Mutual accountability

We encourage each other on training, recovery, diet.

Weekly training

River Valley

long runs, weighted-pack walks

stairs, steady endurance sets

Bukit Timah

vertical repeats with pack

Grouped by fitness. On the mountain, multiple guides so everyone has company at a comfortable pace.

What a weekly commitment looks like

Lightentry~3 hr/wk
  • 1 group session + solo MWF
  • Solo: easy jog every morning or evening, no pack
  • Sat group: Bukit Timah, 2 repeats with 5kg pack (~2 hr)
Standard~5–6 hr/wk
  • 2 group sessions + 1 solo
  • Tue PM: Kallang stadium, stairs and endurance (~1 hr)
  • Sat AM: Bukit Timah, 3–4 repeats with 8kg pack (~2.5 hr)
  • Sun or solo: long PCN walk/run, 90 min
Heavypre-climb block~8–10 hr/wk
  • 3 group + 2 solo
  • Adds a second hill day, longer pack carries, back-to-back weekends

Rinjani — our first trip

Fri 10 – Wed 15 Jul 2026 6 days / 5 nights Fly Scoot SIN ⇄ LOP Dates tentative

Our first climb — Rinjani plus the Gili Islands to finish. Out on the Fri 10 Jul Scoot flight, summit Sun 12 Jul, home on the Wed 15 Jul return. Dates are tentative until we book.

Day 1 · Fri 10 Jul — Arrival → Sembalun
Pick-up from Lombok airport, transfer to Sembalun village, hotel check-in, free time and trekking briefing.
Day 2 · Sat 11 Jul — Trek to Crater Rim
Breakfast, start trekking from Sembalun (1,150m) through savanna and forest, lunch on the trail, reach Crater Rim (2,639m), sunset over Segara Anak Lake, dinner and camp.
Day 3 · Sun 12 Jul — Summit → Lake & Hot Spring
Wake ~2:00 AM for the summit push to 3,726m, sunrise from the top, back to camp for breakfast, trek down to Segara Anak Lake, relax at the hot springs, dinner and camp by the lake.
Day 4 · Mon 13 Jul — Lake → Torean → Senaru
Breakfast at the lake, trek down the scenic Torean route (waterfalls and jungle) to Torean village, transfer to Senaru, hotel check-in and rest.
Day 5 · Tue 14 Jul — Waterfalls → Gili Islands
Morning at Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep waterfalls, transfer to the harbour, boat to the Gili Islands, snorkeling over coral reefs and turtles, hotel check-in and free time.
Day 6 · Wed 15 Jul — Gili → Home
Breakfast, boat back to the Lombok mainland, transfer to the airport, fly home.

Baseline

For grouping, not gatekeeping. About finishing safely, not racing.

  • Resting HR (e.g., 52)
  • Bukit Timah round trip, no pack (e.g., comfortable in 65 min)
  • 100 floors at Pinnacle@Duxton with 5kg pack (how it felt)
  • How far you can walk/run without strain in 12 minutes (e.g., 2.5 km)
  • Longest trek in last 2 years (e.g., 4-day hike in Nepal, 2024)
  • Weekly cardio hours (e.g., 4 hr)
  • Altitude experience above 3,000 m (e.g., once, 4,200 m)
  • Injuries (e.g., minor knee strain, managed)
