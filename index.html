164 Trek

Train weekly. Climb together.

A trekking group for walking trails, weekly training, and safe climbs. We pick the next mountain as a group. If you're keen to join us, just contact us.

Design principles

No falling risk

Walking trails only. Altitude, scree, weather, mud are fine. Ropes, exposed ridges, and glaciers are out.

Start small

One mountain first, then the group decides what's next.

Prepared, not rushed

We build the fitness to finish comfortably and enjoy the whole way up.

Mutual accountability

We encourage each other on training, recovery, diet, and showing up.

Weekly training

River Valley

long runs, weighted-pack walks

Stadium

stairs, steady endurance sets

Bukit Timah

vertical repeats with pack

Grouped by fitness. On the mountain, multiple guides so everyone has company at a comfortable pace.

What a weekly commitment looks like

Lightentry ~3 hr/wk
  • 1 group session + light solo training
  • Solo: easy jog or walk, no pack
  • Group: Bukit Timah, 2 repeats with 5kg pack
Standard ~5–6 hr/wk
  • 2 group sessions + 1 solo session
  • Stadium stairs and endurance work
  • Bukit Timah, 3–4 repeats with 8kg pack
  • Long PCN walk or run, around 90 minutes
Heavypre-climb block ~8–10 hr/wk
  • 3 group sessions + 2 solo sessions
  • Second hill day
  • Longer pack carries
  • Back-to-back weekend training

Rinjani, July 10–15

A 6D / 5N Rinjani and Gili trip built around training, steady climbing, and proper recovery after the mountain.

Day 1 · Fri 10 Jul · Arrival, Sembalun
Airport pick-up, drive to Sembalun, hotel check-in, trekking briefing, and group prep.
Day 2 · Sat 11 Jul · Trek to Crater Rim
Sembalun up through savanna and forest to Crater Rim. Sunset over Segara Anak Lake. Camp overnight.
Day 3 · Sun 12 Jul · Summit, Lake, Hot Spring
Early summit push for sunrise. Descend to Segara Anak Lake, hot springs, and camp.
Day 4 · Mon 13 Jul · Torean to Senaru
Descend through the Torean route with jungle, river sections, and waterfall views. Hotel in Senaru.
Day 5 · Tue 14 Jul · Waterfalls and Gili
Visit Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep waterfalls, then boat to Gili for snorkeling, massage, or recovery time.
Day 6 · Wed 15 Jul · Home
Boat back to Lombok, airport transfer, and flight home.

How we coach each other

Light diet check-ins

Baseline

For grouping, not gatekeeping. About finishing safely, not racing.

  • Resting heart rate
  • Bukit Timah round trip, no pack
  • 100 floors with 5kg pack
  • 12-minute walk or run distance
  • Longest trek in the last 2 years
  • Weekly cardio hours
  • Altitude experience above 3,000m
  • Any injuries or health concerns
