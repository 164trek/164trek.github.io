Routes

The two routes

Both routes share the same start and the same summit. The traverse keeps going and walks out the far side at Kuala Tahan, with a boat on the last day.

Summit · 4D3N

Merapoh to Merapoh

Merapoh (Sungai Relau)› Kuala Juram (4x4)› Kem Kor› Kubang› Padang› Summit 2,187 m› Same way back to Merapoh
Full traverse · 7D6N

Merapoh to Kuala Tahan

Merapoh (Sungai Relau)› Kuala Juram (4x4)› Kem Kor› Kubang› Padang› Summit 2,187 m› Kem Gedung› Wray's Camp› Kuala Teku› Kuala Tahan› Boat down the Tembeling River

Route reference and camp names from gunungbagging.com and Wikipedia. Camp order can change with weather and water levels on the day.
