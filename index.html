Day by day

6D5N itinerary

Times are a guide. The mountain, weather, group pace, and local team may adjust the exact flow. The idea is to keep the trip steady, safe, and enjoyable.

Day 1

Singapore to Lombok, transfer to Sembalun

07:35 Fly from Singapore to Lombok. 10:25 Arrive in Lombok, airport pickup, drive to Sembalun, check in, rest, and trek briefing in the evening.

Day 2

Sembalun to Crater Rim

07:00 Breakfast and final gear check. 08:00 Start trekking from Sembalun through open savanna and forest sections. Reach Crater Rim in the afternoon, watch sunset, dinner, and camp.

Day 3

Summit, Segara Anak Lake, hot springs

02:00 Summit push. 06:00 Sunrise near the summit. Descend back to camp for breakfast, then continue down to Segara Anak Lake and hot springs. Camp near the lake area.

Day 4

Torean route to Senaru

07:00 Breakfast and pack up. Trek out through the Torean route with jungle, river views, and waterfalls along the way. Arrive in Senaru, shower, hotel, proper rest.

Day 5

Waterfalls and Gili Island

09:00 Visit Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep waterfalls. Transfer to the harbor and take the boat to Gili. Afternoon is for snorkeling, island walk, sunset, massage if you want, and slow recovery.

Day 6

Gili to Lombok, fly home

Morning Breakfast, easy beach time if schedule allows, boat back to Lombok, airport transfer, and flight back to Singapore.

Training

Train at your pace. Show up better prepared.

You do not need to be the fastest person. You just need to be honest about your current fitness, train consistently, and join the right pace group. Slow, normal, and fast pace are all welcome.

Group training

For people who want accountability and company. We use simple sessions that actually help for a mountain.

Bukit Timah Weekend morning, inside 7am to 7pm reserve hours
River Valley Evening walk or run, Singapore River Promenade has lighting from 7pm to 7am
Kallang area Evening stairs or endurance sets, exact spot depends on access

Solo training

For weekdays when you cannot meet the group. Keep it simple and consistent.

Daily easy walk or jog 30 to 45 minutes
Weighted pack walk 1 to 2 times weekly, start light
Stairs or hill repeats 1 time weekly, do not destroy your knees

FAQ

Simple answers before you message us.

Do I need to be fast?

No. Slow, normal, and fast pace are welcome. We just need to know your level so we can group people properly.

Is this beginner friendly?

It can be, but Rinjani is still a serious trek. You should train before going. If you are not sure, message us and be honest about your fitness.

Return flight, hotels, transfers, Gili boat, guide, porters, camping gear, trek meals, snorkeling, park fees, permits, and completion certificate.

What is not included?

Personal expenses, meals outside the trek, checked baggage if needed, optional upgrades, and extra tips if you personally want to give more.
