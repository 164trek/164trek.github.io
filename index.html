Tentative Itinerary

Gunung Tahan via Merapoh

Day 1 — 20 July 2026
8:00 AM – Register at Taman Negara Sungai Relau Merapoh office
9:00 AM – 10:00 AM – Check and declare belongings/equipment
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM – 4x4 transfer to Kuala Juram
11:00 AM – 11:30 AM – Briefing by guide
11:30 AM – 2:30 PM – Trek Kuala Juram to Kuala Luis / packed lunch
2:30 PM – 6:00 PM – Trek Kuala Luis to Kem Kor / overnight

Day 2 — 21 July 2026
7:00 AM – Breakfast
8:00 AM – 1:00 PM – Trek Kem Kor to Kem Kubang / lunch
2:00 PM – 6:00 PM – Trek Kem Kubang to Kem Botak / overnight

Day 3 — 22 July 2026
6:00 AM – Trek to summit / summit view / breakfast
10:00 AM – 11:00 AM – Trek summit back to Kem Botak
11:00 AM – 6:00 PM – Trek Kem Botak to Kem Kor / overnight

Day 4 — 23 July 2026
7:00 AM – Breakfast
8:00 AM – 1:00 PM – Trek Kem Kor to Kuala Juram / lunch
2:00 PM – 3:00 PM – 4x4 transfer back to Sungai Relau
3:00 PM – Check belongings, check out / expedition ends

Itinerary is for reference only and may change depending on weather, trail condition, safety, and group pace. The guide may adjust the plan if conditions are unsafe.
