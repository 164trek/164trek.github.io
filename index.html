Itinerary

July 10–15, 2026

Here is the simple plan for each day.

Day 1 — Friday, 10 July

Arrive in Lombok and go to Sembalun

We fly from Singapore to Lombok. After arrival, we transfer to Sembalun, check in at the hotel, do the trek briefing, check our gear, and rest.

Day 2 — Saturday, 11 July

Trek from Sembalun to the crater rim

We start the trek from Sembalun. The trail goes through open savanna and mountain paths. We sleep near the crater rim with views of Segara Anak Lake.

Day 3 — Sunday, 12 July

Summit, lake, and hot spring

We start early for the summit push to Mount Rinjani at 3,726 meters. After the summit, we go down to the lake and hot spring area to rest.

Day 4 — Monday, 13 July

Descend through Torean to Senaru

We take the Torean route down. Expect jungle, river sections, valley views, and waterfall scenery. We finish the trek and sleep in Senaru.

Day 5 — Tuesday, 14 July

Waterfalls and Gili Island

We visit the waterfalls, then take the boat to Gili Island. This is the recovery day: beach time, snorkeling, food, and optional massage.

Day 6 — Wednesday, 15 July

Return to Lombok and fly home

We take the boat back to Lombok, transfer to the airport, and fly back to Singapore.

The climb

What the route feels like

Up to the crater rim, early push to the summit, down to the lake, then out through Torean.
