Rinjani + Gili Island

6D5N trek from Singapore to Mount Rinjani and Gili Island.

6–11 August 2026, tentative
Reserve your slot now!
Itinerary

6 days, 5 nights

Day 1

Thursday, 6 August 2026

Arrive in Lombok. Transfer to Sembalun. Hotel check-in and trek briefing.

Day 2

Friday, 7 August 2026

Trek from Sembalun to the crater rim. Camp overnight.

Day 3

Saturday, 8 August 2026

Early summit push. Descend to the lake and hot spring. Camp overnight.

Day 4

Sunday, 9 August 2026

Descend through Torean to Senaru. Hotel stay.

Day 5

Monday, 10 August 2026

Visit waterfalls. Boat to Gili Island. Snorkeling and recovery time.

Day 6

Tuesday, 11 August 2026

Boat back to Lombok. Airport transfer for departure.
