About the trip

Train together. Climb safe. Enjoy the trail.

This is for people who want a real mountain experience, not just another vacation. We prepare before the trip, climb at a steady pace, and finish with island recovery after the mountain.

Why Rinjani?

Mount Rinjani stands at 3,726 meters and gives you a full adventure: crater rim, summit, lake, hot spring, jungle descent, waterfalls, and island recovery.

Why join 164 Trek?

You do not have to figure everything out alone. We organize the route, logistics, training, and group flow so you can focus on showing up prepared.

Itinerary

6 days, 5 nights

A complete mountain-to-island route from Singapore to Lombok, Rinjani, Senaru, and Gili.

Day 1
Fri, 10 Jul

Arrival in Lombok, transfer to Sembalun

Fly from Singapore to Lombok. Airport pick-up, transfer to Sembalun, hotel check-in, trek briefing, gear check, and rest before the climb.

Day 2
Sat, 11 Jul

Sembalun to Crater Rim

Start the trek from Sembalun through savanna and mountain trail. Reach the crater rim for sunset views over Segara Anak Lake. Overnight camping.

Day 3
Sun, 12 Jul

Summit, Lake, and Hot Spring

Early summit push to Mount Rinjani at 3,726 meters. Descend to the crater lake and hot spring area for recovery and overnight camp.

Day 4
Mon, 13 Jul

Torean route to Senaru

Descend through the Torean route with jungle, river sections, valley views, and waterfall scenery. Transfer to Senaru hotel for proper rest.

Day 5
Tue, 14 Jul

Waterfalls and Gili Island

Visit Sendang Gile and Tiu Kelep waterfalls, then take the boat to Gili Island. Snorkeling, beach time, optional massage, and recovery after the trek.

Day 6
Wed, 15 Jul

Return to Lombok, fly home

Boat back to Lombok, airport transfer, and flight back home. End of the Rinjani + Gili Island trip.

FAQ

Before you join

Simple answers before you message us.

Do I need trekking experience?

You do not need to be an expert, but you should be willing to train. Rinjani is physically demanding, especially summit day.

Is this a race?

No. The goal is to climb safely, stay steady, and enjoy the trail. We train so the climb feels better, not so we can rush through it.

Will there be training before the trip?

Yes. 164 Trek is built around training together before the mountain. We will share weekly training details with interested participants.

Can I join alone?

Yes. You can join solo. The goal is to build a small group where people can train, climb, and travel together.

How do I reserve a slot?

Message us on WhatsApp using the reserve button. We will share the next steps, availability, and payment details.
