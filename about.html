Money transparency

Every penny collected for the trip will be tracked clearly.

The money goes to real trip costs such as guides, admin, permits, local transport, park fees, accommodation, boats, and other trip expenses.

If there is surplus after the trip costs are covered, it will either be returned to the group or invested into the next trek.
