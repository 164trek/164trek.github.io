Money transparency

Every penny tracked.

Every penny collected for the trip will be tracked clearly. The money goes to real trip costs, and participants can ask where it is being spent.

  • Guide fees
  • Admin and coordination
  • Permits and park fees
  • Local transport
  • Accommodation
  • Boats and transfers
  • Trip logistics
  • Emergency buffer

If there is surplus after the trip costs are covered, it will either be returned to the group or invested into the next trek.
