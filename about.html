We started 164 Trek as a small Singapore-based trekking group for people who want to train together and travel to trails worth preparing for.

We keep groups small, work with local guides and operators, and explain costs before collecting payment. Train together. Climb safe. Enjoy the trail.

Costs

What the cost covers

This is for the July 2026 Gunung Tahan trek. Most of the cost is the local guide package and ground expenses. Other trips will show their own numbers before payment.

Guide feesIncluded
Licensed mountain guide for the whole route.
Operator feesIncluded
Booked through a licensed Taman Negara operator.
PermitsIncluded
Park entry, camping, and camera fees.
TransportIncluded
4x4 transfer, plus a boat on the final day for the traverse.
AccommodationCamping
Tents on the trail (flysheet and groundsheet). No hotel nights on this trek.
Food and campingIncluded
All meals on the trail, cooked by a chef. Tent provided.
Support crewPartly
Guide and chef are included. A personal porter is optional and not included.
Trip logisticsIncluded
Park briefing, route coordination, and on-mountain support.
Insurance and certificateIncluded
Trek insurance and a summit certificate.
Getting to Malaysia and MerapohN/A
You arrange and pay for your own way to the trailhead. By land or air is up to you. We travel by land.

Not included on this trek: your way to Malaysia and Merapoh, personal expenses, and an optional porter.
