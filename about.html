Where the money goes

Money collected for the trip will be tracked clearly. It will be used for real trip costs.

  • Guide fees
  • Admin and coordination
  • Permits and park fees
  • Local transport
  • Accommodation
  • Boats and transfers
  • Trip logistics
  • Emergency buffer

Any surplus will either be returned to participants or used for the next trek, with the group informed clearly.
