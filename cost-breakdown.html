How transparency works

Every penny collected is tracked. The live sheet shows roughly where the money goes and will be updated as the trip gets closer.

After the trip, actual costs will be shared with participants. If there is unused buffer, it will either be returned to participants or rolled into the next trek.

Personal participant details are not included in the public sheet.
