How this estimate works

The live sheet shows the current working cost basis for the trip. Final numbers may change if the group size, exchange rate, or supplier costs change before departure.

The public estimate is based on a small-group assumption. If more people join, the shared cost per person can come down.

After the trip, actual costs will be shared with participants. If there is unused buffer, it will either be returned or rolled into the next trek.
