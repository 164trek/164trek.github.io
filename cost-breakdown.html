What the price covers

Most of the cost is the guide package and ground costs

Both options include a licensed mountain guide, all permits and camping and camera fees, 4x4 transfer (plus a boat on the final day for the traverse), all meals cooked by a chef on the trail, a tent, and insurance with a summit certificate.

Not included: your way to Malaysia and Merapoh, personal expenses, and an optional porter.

How this estimate works

These are the working prices per person by group size. Final numbers may change if the group size, exchange rate, or supplier costs change before departure.

After the trip, actual costs are shared with participants. If there is unused buffer, it is either returned or rolled into the next trek.
